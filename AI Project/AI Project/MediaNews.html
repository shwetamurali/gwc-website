Media News

With the rise of AI in everyday objects such as Siri on iPhone, Amazon Alexa, and more, AI has become something that is accessible to everyone, not just to computer scientists. The media has had mixed responses to this, with some regarding its positive effects on making techonology and more accessible to people, while others appear fearful of its negative repercussions. For example, in March 2018, there were reports across the nation of Amazon's Alexa emitting an evil sounding laugh even while turned off. With events like this occurring, some people will always criticize artificial intelligence.
